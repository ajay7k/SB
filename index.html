Welcome!

Empowering Education & Outreach

At SpaceBud, we aim to revolutionize STEM education through digital learning tools, academic mentoring, science kits, and hands-on experiences. We build future-ready minds with a strong foundation in space and atmospheric sciences.

Advanced Space Weather Forecasting

We deliver real-time space weather monitoring and AI-powered forecasts to protect infrastructure and critical systems. Our services benefit aviation, satellite operators, energy sectors, and defense agencies.

Geospatial Innovation

With cutting-edge remote sensing and GIS analytics, SpaceBud empowers sustainable planning, disaster management, environmental conservation, and smart city development across India and beyond.

Space Innovation & Technology

From satellite and UAV design to launch services and orbital sustainability, SpaceBud is paving the way for India's leadership in new-age space technology. We build systems for Earth, orbit, and beyond.
